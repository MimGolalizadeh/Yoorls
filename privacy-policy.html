Privacy Policy for Yoorls

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

Introduction

Yoorls ("we," "us," "our," or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles your information.

Data Collection and Usage

Yoorls collects and stores the following information:

  • URLs: Web addresses that you save through the Extension
  • Categories: Custom categories you create to organize your URLs
  • Theme Preferences: Your choice of light or dark theme settings
  • Display Preferences: Your preferences for how to display and sort URLs

Data Storage

All data collected by Yoorls is stored locally in your browser using the Chrome storage API. This means:

  • Your data remains on your device
  • We do not transmit your data to external servers
  • We do not have access to your saved URLs or personal data
  • Your data is private to your browser profile

Data Security

Your data is stored securely within your browser's local storage. The Extension does not establish any external network connections to transmit user data.

User Control

You have full control over your data:

  • You can delete any URL or category at any time
  • You can clear all data by uninstalling the Extension or clearing your browser data
  • You can manage your theme and display preferences at any time

Permissions

Yoorls requests the following permissions:

  • storage: To save your URLs, categories, and preferences locally
  • activeTab: To access information about the current tab when you save a URL
  • scripting: To inject scripts needed for Extension functionality

Third-Party Services

Yoorls does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services. We do not share your data with any external parties.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to the Extension. Your continued use of Yoorls following the posting of revised Privacy Policy terms means that you accept and agree to the changes.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the Extension's privacy practices, you may contact us through the Chrome Web Store extension page.
